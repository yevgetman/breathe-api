What is Breathe API?

Breathe API gives you instant access to air quality information for any location in the world. We combine data from government monitoring stations, community sensors, and atmospheric models to provide the most accurate, hyper-local air quality data available.

Why Air Quality Matters

Air quality affects everyone's daily life. Poor air quality can impact your health, outdoor activities, and overall well-being. Whether you're planning a jog, managing asthma, or just want to know if it's safe to open the windows, our API provides the information you need to make informed decisions.

Why Multiple Sources?

Different data sources have different strengths. Government stations are highly reliable but sparsely located. Community sensors provide hyper-local data but can be less consistent. Atmospheric models fill in gaps but may lack precision. By combining all these sources intelligently, we give you the most accurate picture possible.
